Why change from WishedArts to Locurb?

Though we were proud for the former, metamorphosis was required, to appeal to greater masses and to future-proof this tight-knit community. We decided on this nonsensical name because :

  • We want to bring together loc(alized) and urb(an) communities.
  • We plan to further optimize our strategies for an effective online drop-in replacement for curb(side) stalls and lo(wer) the barrier of entry for independent sellers and makers for financial freedom.
  • We strive to be a friendly-neighbourhood platform.

What is Locurb?

Locurb is an e-commerce platform, created solely for the purpose of providing a stage to all the artists/artisans, small businesses , etc to showcase their talent through their products.
Locurb takes care of the delivery (Delhivery, ShipRocket, XpressBees, DTDC, BlueDart, etc), payments (Credit/Debit Cards, UPI payments , PayPal) and promotions (Facebook, Instagram, Google Ads, Youtube, etc) on their behalf.

Where is Locurb?

Locurb is based in Kolkata, West Bengal, India, Asia, Earth, Solar System, Milkyway Galaxy; with having its products deliverable to more than 30000+ pincodes in India.

Why does Locurb exist?

Locurb is there to remind everyone, everyday, that don’t let any of your talents go unnoticed and unpraised, so just follow your passion and let your skills speak on your behalf.

What makes Locurb tick?

Because Locurb :

  • don't have any hidden charges whatsoever for sellers/makers/hackers.
  • have transparent pricing for peace of mind for customers and sellers alike.
  • is private by design, requiring minimal information from customers, most of which are regularly purged.
  • have extended privacy for the sellers, so that they can sell with ease.
  • boasts friendly customer and seller support.
